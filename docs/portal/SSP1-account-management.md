## Create account

If you are not registered for an account on the platform, you can sign up by selecting *Register a new account* on the *Welcome* page:

![Welcome Page](/images/self_service_welcome1.png)

The *_Registration_* screen allows for basic details, such as username, email address and password to be completed. Alternatively you can use Social Media accounts to set up your account.

![Register](/images/self_service_register.png)

## Log in

Once you have created your account, you can sign in from the *_Welcome_* page, either by using your platform username and password or by using your Social Media accounts.

[Sign in](/images/self_service_sign_in.png)

## Reset Password

On the *Sign in* page you can also set a *Remember me* option and *Reset your password*.

![Reset password](/images/self_service_reset_password.png)

## Settings

![Account menu](/images/self_service_account_menu.png)

When you are signed into your account, you can change your name, email and language settings from the *Account* menu, *Settings* option.

![Settings](/images/self_service_user_settings.png)

## Change password

To change your password, you must be logged in. Go to the *Settings* option on the *Account* menu and select *Change your password*.

![Change Password](/images/self_service_change_password.png).

## Sign out

The *Sign out* option on the *Account* menu takes you to back to *Welcome* page.
